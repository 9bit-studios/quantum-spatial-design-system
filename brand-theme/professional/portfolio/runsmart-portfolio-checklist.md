## Image Export Specifications

### For PDF Portfolio:
- **Resolution:** 300 DPI minimum
- **Format:** JPG or PNG (PNG for graphics with transparency)
- **Color Space:** RGB (for screen viewing)
- **Max Width:** 2400px (for 8" at 300 DPI)
- **File Size:** Compress to keep each image under 2MB

### For Web Portfolio:
- **Resolution:** 150 DPI (web optimized)
- **Format:** JPG for photos, PNG for graphics
- **Max Width:** 1920px
- **File Size:** Optimize to 200-500KB per image
