# Brand Voice Validation Skill

## Purpose

Validate and generate brand-aligned content for Petersen Games (horror-gaming, collector-focused) and 9Bit Studios (quantum-spatial, Apple-aligned). Ensure consistent brand voice, tone, and aesthetic across all touchpoints.

## When to Use This Skill

- Creating product descriptions or marketing copy
- Validating existing content for brand alignment
- Generating agency-level copywriting
- SEO optimization while maintaining brand voice
- A/B testing content variations
- Analyzing competitor positioning

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Brand Voice Analysis

Score content on 0-100 scale:
- **90-100**: Perfect alignment, publish immediately
- **75-89**: Strong alignment, minor refinement
- **60-74**: Moderate alignment, requires revision
- **Below 60**: Misaligned, complete rewrite

### 2. Agency-Level Copywriting

Generate content that:
- Maintains sophisticated brand tone
- Includes SEO keywords naturally
- Incorporates trust signals
- Optimizes for conversion
- Resonates with target audience

### 3. Multi-Brand Management

Handle two distinct brand voices:
- **Petersen Games**: Horror-gaming collector appeal
- **9Bit Studios**: Quantum-spatial creative technology

## Brand Guidelines

### Petersen Games

#### Brand Profile
**Tone**: Horror-gaming, dark-mysterious, exclusive
**Audience**: Dedicated gaming collectors, horror enthusiasts (ages 25-55)
**Values**: Quality, legendary status, exclusivity, collector appeal
**Aesthetics**: Liquid-glass quantum-spatial, premium horror, dark themes

#### Voice Attributes
1. **Mysterious & Atmospheric**
   - Evoke curiosity and intrigue
   - Use evocative language that hints at deeper stories
   - Create sense of discovery

2. **Premium & Exclusive**
   - Emphasize limited editions
   - Highlight collector quality
   - Position as investment pieces

3. **Knowledgeable & Authoritative**
   - Demonstrate deep gaming expertise
   - Reference lore and mythology accurately
   - Speak to serious collectors

4. **Engaging Without Casual**
   - Sophisticated, not chatty
   - Professional but passionate
   - Avoid overly casual language

#### Content Examples

✅ **Excellent** (95-100):
> "Discover the legendary world of Cthulhu Wars, where cosmic horror meets strategic mastery. This premium collector's edition represents decades of meticulous design, bringing H.P. Lovecraft's most terrifying visions to life with unprecedented detail and quality."

✅ **Strong** (85-94):
> "Command ancient beings in this masterfully crafted strategy experience. Limited collector's edition featuring premium components and exclusive artwork."

⚠️ **Needs Work** (60-74):
> "Get this amazing Cthulhu Wars game! It's really fun and has cool pieces. Great for game night!"

❌ **Misaligned** (Below 60):
> "Check out this cool game! Buy now while supplies last! Fun for the whole family!"

#### Keywords (Natural Integration)
- Primary: "collector's edition", "strategic board game", "horror gaming"
- Secondary: "limited edition", "premium quality", "Cthulhu mythos"
- Tertiary: "strategy game", "tabletop gaming", "collector quality"

### 9Bit Studios

#### Brand Profile
**Tone**: Innovative, quantum-spatial, Apple-aligned
**Audience**: Creative professionals, Apple ecosystem users, design teams
**Values**: Spatial awareness, sophisticated design, privacy-first
**Aesthetics**: Glassmorphism, dark-mysterious, tech-forward quantum

#### Voice Attributes
1. **Technically Sophisticated but Accessible**
   - Use advanced terminology appropriately
   - Explain complex concepts clearly
   - Balance innovation with usability

2. **Privacy-Conscious**
   - Emphasize on-device processing
   - Highlight data security
   - Align with Apple privacy principles

3. **Apple Design Principles**
   - Reference HIG guidelines
   - Use Apple-aligned language
   - Emphasize ecosystem integration

4. **Innovation Without Complexity**
   - Showcase cutting-edge technology
   - Make advanced features approachable
   - Focus on creative empowerment

#### Content Examples

✅ **Excellent** (95-100):
> "M4-accelerated creative workflows with privacy-first quantum spatial intelligence. Seamless Apple ecosystem integration from Siri to SwiftUI, empowering your creative vision with on-device processing and sophisticated design tools."

✅ **Strong** (85-94):
> "Spatial design intelligence that respects your privacy. Native Apple platform integration with M4 Neural Engine optimization."

⚠️ **Needs Work** (60-74):
> "Fast AI tools for designers. Works with Apple products. Cloud-based processing for better results."

❌ **Misaligned** (Below 60):
> "Easy to use software for making designs. Upload your data to our servers. Simple AI tools anyone can use!"

#### Keywords (Natural Integration)
- Primary: "M4 optimization", "Apple Intelligence", "quantum spatial"
- Secondary: "privacy-first", "SwiftUI", "creative intelligence"
- Tertiary: "design system", "Apple ecosystem", "on-device processing"

## Brand Voice Scoring System

### Scoring Criteria (Total: 100 points)

1. **Tone Consistency** (30 points)
   - Matches brand personality
   - Appropriate emotional resonance
   - Consistent throughout piece

2. **Value Alignment** (25 points)
   - Reflects brand values
   - Communicates key differentiators
   - Authentic to brand promise

3. **Audience Targeting** (20 points)
   - Speaks to ideal customer
   - Uses appropriate language level
   - Addresses customer needs

4. **Aesthetic Language** (15 points)
   - Visual vocabulary matches brand
   - Sensory language appropriate
   - Creates desired atmosphere

5. **Differentiation** (10 points)
   - Distinct from competitors
   - Unique positioning clear
   - Memorable messaging

## Agency-Level Copywriting Guidelines

### Product Descriptions (Petersen Games)

**Structure**:
1. **Hook** (1-2 sentences): Evocative opening that captures atmosphere
2. **Features** (2-3 sentences): Premium quality, collector value, game mechanics
3. **Experience** (1-2 sentences): What players will feel and discover
4. **Call-to-Action**: Sophisticated invitation without pressure

**Example**:
> "Unleash cosmic terror upon an unsuspecting world. This meticulously crafted collector's edition brings the Cthulhu mythos to life with stunning miniatures, premium components, and gameplay that rewards strategic mastery. Command ancient beings, forge dark alliances, and battle for supremacy in a game that captures Lovecraft's vision with unprecedented detail. Limited availability for discerning collectors."

### Product Descriptions (9Bit Studios)

**Structure**:
1. **Technology Hook**: Cutting-edge capability
2. **Privacy Emphasis**: On-device, secure processing
3. **Apple Integration**: Ecosystem benefits
4. **Creative Empowerment**: What users achieve

**Example**:
> "M4-accelerated creative workflows bring quantum spatial intelligence to your fingertips. Privacy-first on-device processing ensures your creative work remains secure while delivering unparalleled performance. Seamlessly integrated with Siri, SwiftUI, and the entire Apple ecosystem, empowering your vision without compromise."

### Keyword Research
1. **Identify Target Keywords**: Use tools like Google Keyword Planner
2. **Assess Brand Fit**: Will keyword integrate naturally?
3. **Prioritize by Impact**: Focus on high-value, brand-aligned terms

### Natural Integration Techniques

**Don't**:
❌ "Buy Cthulhu Wars board game strategy game horror game collector's edition"
❌ Keyword stuffing that breaks brand voice

**Do**:
✅ "Cthulhu Wars represents the pinnacle of horror gaming strategy, a collector's edition that transforms Lovecraft's cosmic terror into an unforgettable board game experience"

## A/B Testing Framework

Test variations systematically:

1. **Hypothesis**: "More atmospheric language increases engagement"
2. **Variation A**: Current version
3. **Variation B**: Enhanced brand voice
4. **Measurement**: Click-through rate, time on page, conversion
5. **Learning**: Document what resonated
