Quantum-Spatial Volumetric Pixel System

Advanced 3D/4D volumetric pixels that visually represent different energy states in the quantum-spatial design system. M4-optimized for performance with enhanced visual characteristics.

Heritage State

The Heritage state represents the foundational 8-bit pixel aesthetic with volumetric dimension. This state features clean edges, structured layout, and subtle energy highlights that reference the retro heritage of pixel art while adding modern depth through isometric projection.

Color palette features Heritage Green (#2C5F2D) with Heritage Pixel Green (#3DFF74) accent highlighting, creating a nostalgic yet sophisticated visual language that forms the basis of the Quantum-Spatial design system.

Transitional State

The Transitional state shows the evolution from structured heritage to quantum fluidity. Features softened edges, increased dimensional depth, and animated energy particles that indicate transformation in process. This state blends structure with energy movement.

Uses Dimensional Eggplant (#331F4A) and Subtle Cyan (#5AC8FA) as its primary color scheme, with slight displacement effects and energy particles that begin to break the structured form.

Quantum State

The fully evolved Quantum state represents advanced energy materialization with organic, fluid forms. Features a glowing energy core, orbiting particles, and dimensional ripples that extend beyond the pixel's boundaries. The cubic structure has evolved into a more rounded, organic form.

Uses Quantum Violet (#6A3093) and Rose Energy (#BF4080) with Quantum Pulse Pink (#FF2D55) highlights. Enhanced with animated particle orbits, energy ripples, and distortion effects that create a living, dynamic presence.

Superposition State

The Superposition state represents the simultaneous existence of multiple states, truly embracing quantum principles. Features overlapping forms from all three states (Heritage, Transitional, and Quantum) that shift and morph dynamically, with probabilistic wave patterns and energy transfer between states.

Uses a blend of all color palettes with a brilliant white core that represents pure energy potential. Advanced displacement effects and animated probability waves create a constantly shifting, hyperdimensional presence that transcends traditional UI constraints.
